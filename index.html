Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED — APRIL 2025

Overview

Maven ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications handle your information.

Slapphone — Data Collection

Slapphone does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on-device and requires no account, login, or internet connection.

Permissions Used

Slapphone accesses only the following device features:

  • Accelerometer — to detect physical impact on the device
  • Vibration — to provide haptic feedback scaled to impact intensity
  • Audio — to play sound effects in response to detected motion

No data from these sensors is stored, transmitted, or shared at any point.

No Third-Party Services

Slapphone does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

No Internet Access

Slapphone does not require or use an internet connection. All processing happens locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data from any user, this applies universally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
